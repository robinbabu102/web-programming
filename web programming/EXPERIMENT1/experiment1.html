COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING,VADAKARA
The College of Engineering Vadakara (CEV) is an engineering college in
Kozhikode district of Kerala, established in 1999.

The first engineering college under the
Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE),
[1] Thiruvananthapuram
, established by Govt. of Kerala, started functioning in June 1999.
The college is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)
